Instructions to make Beef Patties:
  1. Ingredients:
  2. In a bowl put the beef
  3. The crushed red pepper
  4. The mustard
  5. The worcestershire
  6. The garlic
  7. The parsley
  8. The cayenne pepper
  9. The garlic salt, mix until well blended
  10. Cover the preparation and place it for 2 hours or more in the refrigerator so that all the flavors are integrated
  11. Divide the mixture into four equal portions, make a ball with each one
