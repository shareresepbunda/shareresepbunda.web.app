The ingredients needed to make Creamy Chicken Sipo:
  1. Make ready 1/2 kg cubed chicken breast fillet
  2. Take 1 cup mixed veggies
  3. Take 1 small diced onion
  4. Take 3 cloves chopped garlic
  5. Get 3 pcs fried hotdogs
  6. Take Salt and pepper
  7. Take 1/2 cup heavy cream
  8. Prepare 1/4 cup water
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp flour
  10. Take 1/2 tbsp fish sauce (optional)
  11. Make ready 2 tbsp butter

Instructions to make Creamy Chicken Sipo:
  1. Saute onion and garlic in butter.
  2. Add chicken. Cook for 5 mins. Then add salt pepper fish sauce and water. Cook for another 10 mins.
  3. Add mixed veggies and hotdogs. Stir. Cook for 3 mins.
  4. In a small cup or bowl, take about 3 tbsp of heavy cream and mix in the flour so it doesnt make a lump. Then add in the cream and the flour solution. Simmer for 5 mins.
  5. Serve.
