The ingredients needed to make Chicken Macronies:
  1. Make ready 1 packet Macronies
  2. Get 2 Breast Chicken
  3. Prepare 3 tbs Soya sauce
  4. Get 2 tbs Vinegar
  5. Get 3 Onions chopped
  6. Get 1 Tomatos chopped
  7. Take 1/2 Cabbage chopped
  8. Take 3 tbs Tomato sauce
  9. Get 4 Potato cut in slices
  10. Take Oil as required
  11. Take Water for boiling
  12. Prepare Salt according to you taste
  13. Prepare Red chilli sauce
  14. Make ready 1 tbsp Sokha daniya
  15. Make ready 1 tbsp Zeera
  16. Prepare 1 tbsp Black pepper powder
