The ingredients needed to make Beef Goulash Soup:
  1. Take 200 gr beef
  2. Make ready 1 onion
  3. Take 2 clove garlic
  4. Make ready 1 carrot
  5. Make ready 2 medium size potato
  6. Take 1 cup wheat
  7. Prepare 1 cup olive oil
  8. Get 2 tablespoon flour
  9. Get 1 tablespoon tomato puree
  10. Prepare Salt
  11. Prepare Black pepper
  12. Prepare 1 liter hot water

Steps to make Beef Goulash Soup:
  1. Put the wheat in hot water and wait for 2 hours
  2. Put olive oil in a pot and add onion and carrot, cook for 5 minutes. Later add tomato pure and flour and stir for 1 minute. Add potatoes and meat, cook for 3 minutes more. Season with salt and black pepper.
  3. Lastly add wheat and water, cover the pot and cook 30 minutes. Serve with chopped persley.
