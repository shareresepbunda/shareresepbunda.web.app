The ingredients needed to make Mongolian Beef:
  1. Get 4 Tbsp soy sauce, divided (just make 2 separate bowls)
  2. Make ready 2 tsp sugar, divided
  3. Prepare 2 tsp corn starch, divided
  4. Get 4 tsp dry sherry, divided
  5. Get 4 tsp hoisin sauce, divided
  6. Prepare 2 tsp seasoned rice vinegar, divided
  7. Take 2 tsp chile paste with ginger, divided

  9. Make ready 4 tsp olive oil, divided
  10. Make ready 1 Tbsp ginger, minced (only in 1 bowl)
  11. Prepare 1 Tbsp garlic, minced (only in 1 bowl)
  12. Take 1 # sirloin steak, sliced very thin across grain
  13. Take 16 medium green onions, cut into 2" pieces
  14. Prepare 1/2 # rice noodles

Instructions to make Mongolian Beef:
  1. Make the rice noodles according to package. Set aside. Slice the green onions into 2" pieces. Use 2 separate small bowls - Add 1T soy sauce, 1 tsp sugar, 1 tsp cornstarch, 2 tsp sherry, 2 tsp hoisin sauce, 1 tsp rice vinegar, 1 tsp chile paste, and 1/4 tsp salt to each bowl. Now to 1 bowl, add 1T minced garlic and 1T minced ginger.
  2. Heat the oil in a large pan on medium heat. Add the bowl of sauce mixture with the garlic and ginger in it to the oil. Stir together and saute for 1 minute. Now add the meat and saute for 2 minutes or until beef is browned. Add the onions and saute 1 minute. Stir in the other bowl of sauce now and saute for 1 minute.
  3. Put a serving of rice noodles on a plate and top with a nice portion of the mongolian beef. This is delicious!
