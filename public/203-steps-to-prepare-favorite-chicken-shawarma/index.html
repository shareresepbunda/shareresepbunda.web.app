Steps to make Chicken shawarma:
  1. In a bowl combine flour,sugar,salt,yeast and oil together add warm water to form soft dough,all it to double it size divide the dough it into 6
  2. Flatten each one to form round shape,grease each side of it using frying pan and put it in a zip lock bag
  3. Cut and wash ur chicken breast add ginger and garlic paste,black pepper,seasoning,white pepper and leave it for 30min
  4. Put little oil in frying pan add the marinated chicken breast and stir fry both side
  5. Mix mayyonase and ketchup in a bowl,apply 1tbsp of it in each bread,add the chicken breast
  6. Add the chopped cabbage,cucumber and carrot which is already mith with mayonnaise and fold the bread
  7. Arrange them in the oven and warm for like 3min and served
