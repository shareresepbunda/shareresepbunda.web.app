The ingredients needed to make Desi style chicken quesadilas:
  1. Make ready 2-3 flour tortillas
  2. Make ready Chicken (boneless tenders)
  3. Take Seasoning salt
  4. Make ready Smoked paprika
  5. Take Peppercorns
  6. Prepare 1 onion
  7. Make ready 1 capsicum
  8. Make ready Shredded cheese monterey jack or cheddar
  9. Take Olive oil
  10. Take Ginger powder
  11. Make ready Avocadoes
  12. Take leaves Coriander

Instructions to make Desi style chicken quesadilas:
  1. Make small cubes of the chicken and mix it well with seasoning salt, smoked paprika, ginger powder, crushed pepper
  2. Dice the onions and capsicums in small cubes
  3. Cook the chicken separately until the flavours come out and also the onions and capsicum
  4. Apply some butter on one side of tortilla and add the cheese, vegetables and chicken stuffing and again more cheese on top as layer and cover the other half and turn over to cook both sides
  5. Prepare some guacomole by smashing the avocadoes and mixing with some onion and coriander paste lastly serve the warm quesadillas with the guacamole
