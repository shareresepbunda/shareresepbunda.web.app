The ingredients needed to make Sesame Beef:
  1. Take Beef (sliced)
  2. Get 1,5 tablespoon sesame oil
  3. Get 3 cloves garlic, minced
  4. Make ready Water
  5. Get Onion, cut into 2 inch lenght
  6. Take White sesame seeds for garnishing
  7. Get To taste beef powder
  8. Make ready To taste black pepper
  9. Prepare Marinade
  10. Take 2 tablespoon dark soy sauce
  11. Prepare 2 table spoon light soy sauce
  12. Get 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  13. Make ready 1 tablespoon rice cooking wine
  14. Take 2 teaspoon sugar
  15. Take 1 tablespoon ginger juice
