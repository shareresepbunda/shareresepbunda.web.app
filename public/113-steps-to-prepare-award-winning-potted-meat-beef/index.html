The ingredients needed to make Potted Meat, (Beef):
  1. Get 1-2/3 pound ground sirloin
  2. Get 1/2 medium onion chopped
  3. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon celery salt
  4. Get 1/2 teaspoon salt
  5. Prepare 1 teaspoon dried marjoram
  6. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon ground white pepper
  7. Take 1/2 teaspoon granulated garlic powder
  8. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  9. Get 2 teaspoon ground Hungarian paprika
  10. Prepare 2 tablespoons extra Virgin olive oil
  11. Prepare 1 teaspoon liquid smoke

Steps to make Potted Meat, (Beef):
  1. Brown the ground sirloin and onions in a pan. Drain the meat and save both the meat and drippings. Let the meat cool.
  2. Gather the spices together
  3. Take a blender and blend the meat into a paste adding oil and drippings as needed.
  4. Do this in batches. Set each batch into a mixing bowl.
  5. When all the meat is like a paste, mix with the spices. Also add the liquid smoke. Knead the meat like dough.
