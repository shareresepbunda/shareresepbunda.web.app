  12. Prepare 2 tbsp Butter

Steps to make Beef lasagna:
  1. In pan add oil onion garlic and saute for 2 mins
  2. Add mince and saute until mince changes color
  3. Add tomatoes tomato ketchup and mix well
  4. Add salt red chilli,black pepper,tomato paste and water and cook for 10 minutes
