The ingredients needed to make Beef Tenderloin Sauce:
  1. Make ready 2 lbs beef tenderloin
  2. Prepare 2 shallots
  3. Make ready 2 cloves garlic(minced)
  4. Prepare Kosher salt(as needed)
  5. Prepare 3 c beef broth
  6. Make ready 1 lb baby portabella mushrooms (sliced)
  7. Prepare 1/3 c red cooking wine
  8. Prepare Equal amount 2 tbs.flour & water for slurry
  9. Prepare 3 slice bacon (chopped)
  10. Take 2 tbs. butter
  11. Make ready 1 tbs. tomato paste
  12. Take Olive oil for cooking
