The ingredients needed to make Chicken poppers:
  1. Prepare 2 chicken breast
  2. Prepare Flour (as you need)
  3. Prepare 2 eggs
  4. Take Ground black pepper
  5. Get Paprika
  6. Take Salt
  7. Get Dried thyme
  8. Get Dried basil
  9. Prepare 3 tablespoon soy sauce
  10. Get 1 tablespoon honey
  11. Make ready 2 cloves garlic
  12. Prepare 100 ml water
  13. Take Red bell pepper

Steps to make Chicken poppers:
  1. Chop chicken. Beat two eggs. Mix flour and spices.
  2. Dredge your chickens in the flour, coat it in beaten eggs and dredge it in flour again. Use flour less at the first time and more at the last time. Then fry them (you can use more oil too)
  3. Mix soy sauce, chopped garlic, honey and water
  4. Spill the mixture into the pan.
  5. Take them out and bake red bell peppers in the same pan
  6. Add peppers on chicken poppers
