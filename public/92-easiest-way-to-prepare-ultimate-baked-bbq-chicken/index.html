The ingredients needed to make Baked BBQ Chicken:
  1. Make ready Garlic
  2. Get Onion
  3. Get Cajun seasoning
  4. Get Original Sweet Baby Ray's BBQ sauce
  5. Take 2 tablespoons margarine
  6. Take Boneless chicken thighs

Easy and healthy Baked BBQ Chicken - You can whip up the sauce, pour it over some chicken breasts, pop it in the. Place chicken breast in a baking dish. Season chicken with salt and pepper, to taste. Spread BBQ sauce on each chicken breast.

Steps to make Baked BBQ Chicken:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Spray a pan (large enough for your amount of chicken) with vegetable oil spray
  3. Season chicken on both sides with garlic, onion, and Cajun seasoning, in the vegetable oil sprayed pan.
  4. Place chicken in oven for 25 minutes
  5. Leave oven on at 350. Take out chicken, add margarine and sweet baby Ray's BBQ sauce over top.
  6. Place back in oven and cook an additional 25-30minutes.
  7. Take out, let it cool down, and serve with a side of veggies and mashed potatoes. Or serve with your own choice of sides.
  8. Enjoy!
