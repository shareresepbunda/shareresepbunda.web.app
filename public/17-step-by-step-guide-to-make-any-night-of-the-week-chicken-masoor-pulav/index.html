The ingredients needed to make Chicken masoor pulav:
  1. Take 1 cup whole masoor dal(here we get both small & big in size) 3/4 cup oil
  2. Take 1 kg chicken,
  3. Prepare 2 spoon curd
  4. Take 1 block maggi cube
  5. Take Masala(1 pod of garlic,1 inch ginger,8 green chillies,1 tsp cuminseeds, 1 cup coriander leaves)
  6. Take 1 sliced onion with 8 black pepper, 5 green cardamom, 2 bay leaves,
  7. Take 1 stick cinnamon, 1 tsp cuminseeds & 1 black cardamom
  8. Prepare 3 cups basmati rice soaked in water for atleast 10 mins
  9. Get Salad(1 cup curd,1 chopped onion,1 chopped tomato,1 green chilli chopped,1 tsp salt & some coriander leaves)
  10. Make ready Some potatoes chopped,2 tbsn salt & 2 cups of water

Steps to make Chicken masoor pulav:
  1. First soak the lentils in warm water(use big size masoor dal) now grind the masala(pic2)slice the onion and remove the whole garam masala.
  2. Now heat oil in a big vessel, then add onion and garam masala and saute till onions are brown.now add the grinded masala,salt and curd.saute few minutes.
  3. Now add the chicken pieces and mix well,now add potatoes with lentils and simmer for 5 mins with closed lid.now add rice with 2 cups of water.check seasoning
  4. Now when most of water is absorbed by rice give a mix,add little water and cover the lid with cloth.simmer on slow flame for 5 to 10 mins and off flame.open the lid after 5 mins(pic2)give a slight mix.pulav is ready
  5. Mix all above ingredients of salad and serve with hot pulav and enjoy(decorate with fried onions and coriander leaves)
