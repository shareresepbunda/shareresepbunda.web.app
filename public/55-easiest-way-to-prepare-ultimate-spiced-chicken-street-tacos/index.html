The ingredients needed to make Spiced Chicken Street Tacos:
  1. Make ready 3 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
  2. Prepare 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1" strips
  3. Make ready Salt and Pepper
  4. Take 2 tsp. chili powder
  5. Take 2 tsp. cumin
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp. garlic powder
  7. Prepare 1/4 tsp. paprika
  8. Prepare 1/4 tsp. cayenne
  9. Take 8 corn or flour tortillas, warmed
  10. Get Toppings:
  11. Take Sour cream, onion, cilantro, tomatoes, shredded Monterey Jack cheese, lime

Steps to make Spiced Chicken Street Tacos:
  1. Cut up chicken in 1 inch strips. Add all spices, garlic and olive oil. Marinate for one hour or overnight.
  2. In a large skillet over medium heat, heat oil. Cook chicken until golden, 6-8 minutes.
  3. Build tacos: In tortillas, layer chicken and desired toppings. Serve with lime wedges.
