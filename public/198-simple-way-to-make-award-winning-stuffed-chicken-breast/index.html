Instructions to make Stuffed Chicken Breast:
  1. Preheat oven to 200 degree celsius or 400 fahrenheit. - - Place chicken breast on a working surface. - - Cover with cling film. - - Using a meat mallet or a rolling pin, gently pound the chicken until even thickness.
  2. Slit a hole lengthwise on the chicken breast. - - Using a fork, gently poke the chicken breast with some holes. This will allow even baking. - - In a small bowl, add paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, pepper and mushroom powder. - - Mix to combine well. Mix, mix, mix.
  3. Rub the chicken well with the spices and seasonings. - - Set aside. - - In another bowl, add cream cheese, parmigiano, mayo, kale, garlic and chili flakes. - - Mix to combine well.
  4. Stuff this cheese mixture into the hole of the chicken breast. - - Transfer onto a baking tray lined with parchment paper. - - Wack into the oven and bake for 20 mins or until browned.
  5. Rotate the tray at halfway mark for evening browning. - - Remove from the oven and transfer onto a serving plate. - - Sprinkle chilies over the top. - - Serve immediately.
