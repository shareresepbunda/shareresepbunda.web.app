  1. Prepare Extreme wellness tortilla shell

  4. Get 1/4 cup chopped onions
  5. Take 1/8 cup chopped red pepper
  6. Make ready 1 tbs olive oil
  7. Take 1 pat butter
  8. Make ready 2 cloves minced garlic
  9. Take 1/2 tsp chipotle garlic seasoning

Steps to make Cast iron chicken quesadillas:
  1. Heat olive oil in cast iron pan medium heat and saut√© onion, red peppers and garlic
  2. Add in chicken and add chipotle garlic seasoning. Mix well and cook until well combined
  3. Remove chicken mixture and set aside in a bowl.
  4. Spread butter on one half of tortilla shell as light as possible.
  5. Place butter side down in the cast iron skillet. And place chicken mixture on half of the tortilla.
  6. Add cheese on top of chicken and fold
  7. Brown one side while the cheese melts then flip over and brown the other side.
