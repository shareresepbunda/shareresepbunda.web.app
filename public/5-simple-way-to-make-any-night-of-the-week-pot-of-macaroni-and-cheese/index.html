The ingredients needed to make Pot of macaroni and cheese:
  1. Take Box macaroni elbow
  2. Get 3 cups cheese shredded sharp and Mozzarella or any of your favorite
  3. Prepare 1/2 gallon milk
  4. Get 1/2 stick butter
  5. Prepare 3 tablespoon flour
  6. Get 1/2 teaspoon salt
  7. Take 1/2 white pepper
  8. Make ready 1/8 teaspoon nutmeg optional
  9. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon dry mustard optional
  10. Prepare 1 tablespoon hot sauce optional
