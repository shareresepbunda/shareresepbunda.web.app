The ingredients needed to make Beef Kidney Stew over Mash:
  1. Take Kidney and soak
  2. Get 3/4 pound beef kidney
  3. Make ready 1 can evaporated milk
  4. Make ready 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  5. Take Stew
  6. Make ready 3 large carrots
  7. Prepare 2 pound eye of round steak
  8. Take 1/2 cup cabernet Sauvignon California, red
  9. Make ready 8 ounces baby portobello mushrooms
  10. Get 1 large onion sliced
  11. Prepare 1 quart beef broth
  12. Take 3 tablespoons butter
  13. Make ready 3 tablespoon all-purpose flour
  14. Take To taste salt
  15. Get To taste ground white pepper
  16. Prepare 1 cup chopped celery
  17. Take 2 cup diced potatoes
  18. Take 1/2 teaspoon thyme
  19. Get 1/2 teaspoon tarragon
  20. Take 1 apple peeled, cored, and diced
  21. Prepare 1 teaspoon granulated garlic powder
  22. Get Mash
  23. Make ready 24 ounces canned evaporated milk
  24. Get 1/2 stick butter
  25. Make ready 1 cup water
  26. Take To taste salt
  27. Prepare As needed water to boil potatoes in
  28. Take Kidney fry
  29. Make ready 1/2 stick butter
  30. Get 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  31. Get To taste salt
  32. Get To taste ground black pepper
Steps to make Beef Kidney Stew over Mash:
  1. Night before remove membrane from outside of kidney. Then cut the kidney in half lengthwise. Then remove white center.
  2. Slice the kidney into bite sized pieces. Add the kidney to a container. Add milk and vinegar. Stir well put into refrigerator overnight. For 2 nights.
  3. Melt the butter and add oil. Add the kidney to a paper towel, pat dry. Fry the kidney in the butter oil mixture. A lot of liquids will come out the kidney pieces. Allow to evaporate.
  4. Drain the milk and rinse. Add salt to a container and add a little room temperature water. Add the kidney stir well and cover with water. Let rest 30 minutes and drain and rinse.
  5. When kidney pieces are done add salt and pepper.
  6. Slice the carrots, mushrooms, apple, celery, dice the potatoes, and slice the onions. Add the vegetable mixture sauté till done.
  7. Add the potatoes before the eye of round steak. Cube the eye of round.
  8. Add the eye of round to the vegetables. Add the spice, salt, and pepper. Stir in cover and stew 15 minutes. Add the wine cook for 15 minutes longer.
  9. Add the flour and cook till the flour is cooked. Add the beef broth and stir till it forms a thin gravy.
  10. Peel the potatoes. Cook the mash. Boil the potatoes in water till fork tender. Drain, mash, and put back into pot. Add milk, water, and butter mash and mix. Salt to taste.
  11. Cook the stew till thickened. Serve over the mash. I hope you enjoy!!!
