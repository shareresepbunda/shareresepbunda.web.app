The ingredients needed to make Blue Cheese Sauce Chicken:
  1. Make ready 1/2 cup heavy cream
  2. Get 5 oz dorblu cheese (or any blue cheese)
  3. Make ready 1 chicken breast
  4. Prepare 1-2 cups common mushrooms (champignon mushrooms)

Steps to make Blue Cheese Sauce Chicken:
  1. Preheat the oven to 392¬∞F
  2. Place the cream on a burner over medium heat and add small pieces of cheese (2-3 oz). Stir constantly to let the cheese dissolve (do not bring to boil)
  3. Meanwhile slice the chicken with long, even strokes into slices about 1/4-inch thick
  4. Chop mushrooms
  5. Place chicken, mushroom and asparagus into a baking dish and pour the cheese the cheese sauce on top. Place on top small pieces of the left cheese
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes until ready
