Instructions to make Tadjin (Beef and vegetables):
  1. In a cooker, put beef, spices and chickpeas with a cup of water
  2. Wash and cut vegetables
  3. After 10 mins of cooking, add vegetables and 3 cups of boiled water then mix well
  4. Close the cooker and cook for 30 mins
