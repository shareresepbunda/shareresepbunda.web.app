The ingredients needed to make Honey Sesame Chicken:
  1. Make ready Chicken breast
  2. Make ready 2 eggs
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp soy souce
  4. Get 1/4 cup cornflour
  5. Make ready 1/4 cup honey
  6. Get 1/4 cup water
  7. Take 1/2 lemon or 1 lime
  8. Prepare 1/4 tsp ngohiong
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp sesame oil
  10. Get 2 tbsp ketchup

Steps to make Honey Sesame Chicken:
  1. Cut the chicken, matinade with egg and soy sauce, set aside
  2. For sauce: mix the remaining ingredients except for corn flour. Add lemon/lime zest and juice
  3. Add corn flour to chicken marinade, and then fry
  4. Bring sauce mixture to boil, simmer for approx. 10 mins until thicken
  5. Toss fried chicken and sauce until combined, sprinkle sesame seeds. Done
