The ingredients needed to make Chicken Manchurian:
  1. Take 2 chicken breast
  2. Prepare 1 egg whisked
  3. Take 2 tbsp flour
  4. Take to taste Salt & pepper
  5. Take to taste Chilli powder
  6. Take Sauce
  7. Take 1 tbl Oil
  8. Get 1 tsp Garlic ginger paste
  9. Take 1/2 green chilli
  10. Make ready 1/2 tsp black pepper
  11. Make ready 1 tsp soya sauce
  12. Take 1 tbsp ketchup
  13. Prepare 1 tbsp Schezwan sauce
  14. Take 1 tbsp sweet chilli sauce
  15. Make ready 1 tsp white vinegar
  16. Prepare 100 ml water add more if you want it saucy
  17. Make ready Spring Onion and pepper optional
  18. Make ready To thicken sauce
  19. Make ready 1 tbsp corn flour mixed with 2tbsp water
