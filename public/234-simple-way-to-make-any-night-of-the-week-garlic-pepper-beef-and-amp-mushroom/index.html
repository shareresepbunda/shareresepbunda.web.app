The ingredients needed to make Garlic Pepper Beef & Mushroom:
  1. Make ready 1/4 kg thinly sliced beef
  2. Take 1/4 cup mushrooms
  3. Get 1 tbsp chopped garlic
  4. Take 1/2 tbsp worcestershire sauce
  5. Get 1/2 tbsp soy sauce
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp baking soda
  7. Take 1/2 tbsp flour
  8. Prepare 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  9. Make ready 1/2 tsp black ground pepper
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp butter
  11. Make ready 1 tbsp oil
  12. Prepare 1/4 cup water
  13. Prepare Salt
  14. Take (You may adjust ingredients according to your liking)

Instructions to make Garlic Pepper Beef & Mushroom:
  1. Marinate the beef in soy sauce, worcestershire, salt, pepper, baking soda, flour, garlic powder for at least 15 mins.
  2. In a pan over medium heat, saute garlic in oil for 1 min. Then add the marinated beef. Mix well and cook for about 5 mins.
  3. Add the mushrooms, butter and water. Mix well. Cook for another 5 minutes or until beef is tender.
