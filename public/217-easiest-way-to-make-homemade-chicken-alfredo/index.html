Instructions to make Chicken Alfredo:
  1. Add seasoning, black pepper, garlic, ginger and oil to the chicken breast
  2. Cook for 8 minutes
  3. In a separate pot, cook pasta for 8 minutes(you can use any pasta of your choice)
  4. Drain the pasta and add chopped onions and garlic in the pot
  5. Add butter and oil on a high-medium heat
  6. Sauté, add milk and one tablespoon of flour
  7. Bring it to a boil and add the cooked pasta
  8. Cook for 3-4 minutes then add chicken breast and freshly chopped parley
  9. Serve hot!
