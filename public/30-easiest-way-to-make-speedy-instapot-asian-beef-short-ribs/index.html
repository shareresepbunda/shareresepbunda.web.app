Instructions to make (Instapot) Asian Beef Short Ribs:
  1. Place Beef Short Ribs into Instapot.
  2. Mix all marinade ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Pour marinade over short ribs.
  4. Cover and pressure cook according to your cooker instructions on manual for 40 minutes.
  5. Let the Instapot aspirate naturally for 15 minutes.
  6. Release pressure according to the cookers direction.
  7. Place Ribs on a baking pan and broil until brown, about 5 minutes or so
  8. Put the marinade over rice.
  9. Enjoy!
