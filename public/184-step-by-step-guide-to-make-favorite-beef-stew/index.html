The ingredients needed to make Beef stew:
  1. Get 1/4 Cup flour
  2. Make ready 1/2 Cup butter
  3. Take 1 Tbls pepper
  4. Get 1 tsp salt
  5. Make ready 2 lbs stew meat
  6. Get 1 chopped Onion
  7. Make ready 2 chopped celery sticks
  8. Make ready 4 chopped carrots
  9. Make ready 1 cubed potatoe
  10. Get 1 tsp salt
  11. Take 2 Tbls pepper
  12. Get 1 tsp celery salt
  13. Prepare 3 Tbls beef broth granules
  14. Take 2 cups water
  15. Prepare 1 can Pettit tomatoes
  16. Prepare 1 Small can tomato paste
  17. Take 1 bay leaf

Steps to make Beef stew:
  1. In the pot your going to cook the stew use 1/4 Cup butter and sauté onion and celery.
  2. Coat the stew meat with 1/4 Cup flour,1 tsp salt, 1 Tbls pepper. In a frying pan and 1/4 Cup butter and fry stew meat until browned.
  3. Chop carrots, potatoe, blend tomatoes, paste, water, beef broth, salt, pepper, celery salt. After onions and celery are sautéed add all other ingredients.
  4. Add bay leaf and stir. Simmer for at least an hour or until carrots are soft. Take bay leaf out before serving.
