The ingredients needed to make Spicy Beef Stew:
  1. Make ready 2 lbs stew beef
  2. Take 1 tbs onion powder
  3. Prepare 1 tbs garlic powder
  4. Get 1 sazon packet
  5. Prepare 1 tbs adobo
  6. Take 2 tbs soy sauce
  7. Take 1 chicken bouillon cube
  8. Make ready 1 tsp oregano
  9. Take 1/4 tsp cumin
  10. Make ready to taste Red pepper flakes
  11. Take 3 tbs tomato paste
  12. Prepare 1-2 tsp light brown sugar
  13. Take 3-4 c water
  14. Take 1 med sweet potato peeled
  15. Make ready 2-3 small white potatoes, peeled
  16. Get 2-3 carrots, peeled
  17. Prepare 1 yellow onion
  18. Get 1/4 red pepper, chopped
  19. Take Olive oil

Steps to make Spicy Beef Stew:
  1. Season beef with red pepper, onion powder, adobo, garlic powder, sazon, soy sauce, chicken bouillon cube, oregano & olive oil. Cover & let marinade 1-2 days.
  2. Heat a pot over medium heat with olive oil. Add beef & brown on each side. Once beef is browned on all sides, add 3-4 c water. (I recommend putting water into marinade bowl so you don't waste marinade).You want water to cover beef. Cover pot & let beef boil 1- 1 1/2 hrs until beef is tender. Check on it occasionally, if water dries up, add more water. Once beef is tender, cut potatoes, carrots & onion.
  3. Add regular potatoes & carrots. Stir, add more water if needed. Let beef & potatoes simmer for 10 minutes. Then add sweet potatoes & onions, stir. After 6 minutes, add red pepper flakes, stir again. Let boil 5 minutes.
  4. Add tomato paste & brown sugar, stir again. Let stew boil until reaching desired thickness. Serve over white rice or with bread.
