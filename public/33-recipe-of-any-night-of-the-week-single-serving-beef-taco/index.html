Instructions to make Single Serving Beef Taco:
  1. First, marinate the beef for at least 1 hour.
  2. After, add in oil to a saucepan and sear the beef on both sides until rare or medium rare.
  3. Take it out and cut into bit size pieces.
  4. In the same pot (don't rinse it) add in the onions and garlic and sautÃ©e until fragrant, add in the remaining marinade, tomato puree, water or stock, lemon juice and season and cook until slightly thick and bubbly.
  5. Then, add in the beef and cook stirring frequently until the beef is cooked to your taste.
  6. For the yoghurt sauce, combine all the ingredients in a small bowl and leave for about 5 minutes to absorb more of the garlic flavour.
