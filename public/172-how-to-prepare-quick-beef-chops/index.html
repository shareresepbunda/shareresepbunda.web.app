The ingredients needed to make Beef chops:
  1. Prepare 1 kg Beef Chops
  2. Prepare 1 tsp White Cumin (Milled & Roasted)
  3. Take 2 tbsp White Vinegar
  4. Prepare 1 1/2 tbsp Milled Ginger Garlic
  5. Make ready 3 Green Chili chopped
  6. Prepare 1 cup Oil
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp Red Chili , milled
  8. Make ready 1 tsp Black Pepper , chopped
  9. Prepare 3 Lemon
  10. Make ready Salt as per taste
  11. Get 1/2 bunch Coriander, for garnishing (Finely Chopped)

Steps to make Beef chops:
  1. Wash chops and put them into a cooking pot with 2 bowl water and all spices, boil them.
  2. When water gets dry take out chops and place them in a dish.
  3. Heat oil in frying pan and put chops in hot oil, when they turn brown take them out and put again in the same cooking pot, boiled before
  4. When all chops are fried, put lemon juice and green Spices from top and cook for 10 minutes.
  5. Tasty chops are ready, serve it with hot naans.
