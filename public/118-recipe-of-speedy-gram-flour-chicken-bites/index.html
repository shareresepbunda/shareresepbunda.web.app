The ingredients needed to make Gram flour chicken bites:
  1. Get Half kg boneless chicken
  2. Take 1 cup gram flour
  3. Make ready 1 teaspoon Salt
  4. Prepare 1 tablespoon crushed red pepper
  5. Take 1 full hand of parsley
  6. Prepare Water as need

Instructions to make Gram flour chicken bites:
  1. Put the gram flour in a bowl add the pepper salt and parsley and add water
  2. Then mix it well you want a little thick mixture
  3. Cut the chicken into 2 inch pieces
  4. Then add all the chicken in that mixture and combine well cover all the chicken then pick one piece of chicken and fry them all
