The ingredients needed to make Spiced Cajun Chicken Pasta:
  1. Make ready 1 rotisserie chicken
  2. Make ready 2-3 peppers chopped, yellow, red orange
  3. Get 1 pound fettuccini noodles, I use flavored noodles like basil
  4. Take 1/2 cup parm cheese
  5. Prepare 1/4 cup white wine
  6. Make ready 1 large lemon / squeeze juice
  7. Make ready 3 tbsp butter
  8. Get 1 /2 tbsp flour
  9. Get 2 small pints of whipping cream or whole milk
  10. Get 1 tbsp Cajun seasoning
  11. Get 1 tbsp red pepper seasoning
  12. Make ready 1 tsp barbere or Indian seasoning
  13. Get 1 tsp nutmeg
  14. Get Salt and pepper generous
  15. Make ready Splash milk if needed to thin sauce
  16. Get 12 grape tomatoes

Steps to make Spiced Cajun Chicken Pasta:
  1. Heat up your iron skillet, add 3 tbsp of butter on med heat.
  2. Boil water for noodles w salt / do not add noodles yet. Get it boiling
  3. Chop all veggies, leave grape tomatoes whole, juice lemon set aside,
  4. Once butter is melting add 1 pint of cream, stir, it will bubble add all your seasoning, stir, add lemon juice, stir. Add white wine, salt and pepper, flour. Stir, add additional pint of cream stir. This should bubble but not burn keep stirring, add milk if needed to thin sauce, you want it like Alfredo sauce. Add more seasoning to taste for spicy flavor. Add parm cheese. Turn down to med low heat/ add veggies, and chicken. Stir let this simmer for 5-8 minutes
  5. Add pasta noodles, boil for 5-8 minutes, strain add a little pasta water, w noodles and oil oil. Set aside
  6. Take your pasta sauce off stove! You are ready to serve sauce over hot noodles!! Add parm cheese to taste!!
