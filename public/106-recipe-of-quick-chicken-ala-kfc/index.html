The ingredients needed to make Chicken Ala Kfc:

  3. Get As needed Flour
  4. Take 2 eggs
  5. Make ready To taste Salt
  6. Take To taste Black pepper

Instructions to make Chicken Ala Kfc:
  1. Mix well the flour, salt and black pepper.
  2. Crack 2 eggs and beat it
  3. Heat wok and add a lot of oil just enough to deep fry the chicken
  4. Take one by one of chicken. Cover it in flour then dip in egg then back to flour.
  5. Deep fry it till it is brown
