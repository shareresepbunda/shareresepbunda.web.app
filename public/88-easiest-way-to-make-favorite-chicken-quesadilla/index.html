The ingredients needed to make Chicken Quesadilla:
  1. Get strips Chicken breast, cut into thin
  2. Make ready Onion, minced
  3. Get 2 tsp paprika powder
  4. Prepare 2 tsp cumin powder
  5. Take 1 tsp garlic powder
  6. Make ready 1 tsp onion powder
  7. Prepare 1 tsp oregano
  8. Make ready Cream cheese
  9. Take Mayonnaise
  10. Get Oil
  11. Prepare to taste Salt and pepper
  12. Make ready Flour tortilla
  13. Prepare Butter
  14. Make ready Monterey Jack and cheddar, shredded

Steps to make Chicken Quesadilla:
  1. Saute onion, then add the chicken breast. Seasoned with paprika, cumin, garlic powder, onion powder,l and oregano. You may add some chili powder if you want a little spicy.
  2. Saute until cooked. Turn off heat then add the cream cheese and mayonnaise. Adjust with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Lightly grease the pan with butter then heat the flour tortilla. Fill half of the tortilla with the fillings, add shredded Monterrey Jack and cheddar then fold. Make sure not to put too much filling so that it will me manageable to fold the tortilla. Slightly brown both sides.
  4. Serve with sour cream or guacamole.
