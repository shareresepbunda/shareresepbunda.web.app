The ingredients needed to make Crockpot Shredded Chicken Paprika:
  1. Take 1 large pack chicken breast
  2. Take 1-28 ounce can crushed tomatoes
  3. Get 1/2 cup red wine
  4. Take 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  5. Make ready 1 tablespoon paprika
  6. Make ready 6 cloves garlic, minced
  7. Get 1 onion, sliced
  8. Take 1 green pepper, sliced
  9. Take White rice, cooked
