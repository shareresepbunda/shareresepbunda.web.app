The ingredients needed to make Beef Stew (v2.0):
  1. Make ready (2.5) Lbs Top or Bottom Round Roast
  2. Take (10-15) Baby Potatoes
  3. Take (4) Carrots ( Skinned and cut into 2 1/2 inch pieces
  4. Prepare Handful Baby Onions
  5. Make ready Handful Cherry Tomatos
  6. Make ready Fresh Thyme
  7. Make ready Fresh Rosemary
  8. Make ready Cloves (4) Garlic
  9. Prepare (2) Ounces Dark Chocolate
  10. Prepare (4) Oz's Tomato Paste
  11. Take (2) Tbsp Flour
  12. Take Whole Bottle Red Wine
  13. Prepare Salt and Pepper

Instructions to make Beef Stew (v2.0):
  1. Cut Roast into Large 2 inch cubes. Add to large bowl.
  2. Salt and pepper to taste. Add thyme and Rosemary. Add potatoes, carrots, onions,Cherry tomatoes and garlic.
  3. Pour in whole bottle of red wine. Covered with plastic wrap, marinade in refrigerator overnight.
  4. Strain whine out into a large bowl and save.
  5. Place the roast and vegetable mixture into a large pot on medium high. Seer until brown.
  6. Re add the red wine. Bring to boil. Add the tomato paste, flour and Dark Chocolate. Stir, turn to low. Cover and let cook 6 hours.
  7. Keep an eye on liquid level. Usually plenty though.
  8. Enjoy!
